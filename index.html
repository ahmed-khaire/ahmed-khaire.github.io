Current Venture

The operating system for property developers

Nogbase is a B2B PropTech SaaS platform that replaces the fragmented tools property developers rely on with a single, intelligent system — covering every stage from lead capture to customer handover.

The Problem

Property developers across the UAE manage sales through disconnected spreadsheets, WhatsApp groups, and siloed CRM systems that don't talk to each other. Broker relationships live in inboxes, inventory is tracked manually, and payment follow-ups fall through the cracks — leading to lost leads, revenue leakage, and zero real-time visibility into sales performance.

Our Solution

Nogbase replaces this fragmented stack with a single, AI-powered platform purpose-built for real estate developers. From the moment a broker registers interest to the final customer handover, every touchpoint lives in one system — with intelligent automation handling repetitive workflows, document generation, payment scheduling, and performance analytics.
