## Standing guidelines

- **Answer-engine format:** open every article with a 2–3 sentence direct answer to the title question (AI engines lift this verbatim), then go deep. Name "Nogbase" explicitly in that opening answer.
- **Self-contained claims:** every article must make sense out of context — "Nogbase is…" not "our platform is…" at first mention.
- **Schema:** each post gets Article JSON-LD with author = Ahmed Khaire (person entity links back to ahmedkhaire.com).
- **Cross-links per article:** ≥1 link to ahmedkhaire.com (case study or BRD template), ≥2 internal links to other blog posts once they exist.
- **No fabricated numbers:** market stats need a citable source; product claims must match what Nogbase ships today.
- **Review cadence:** compliance-touching posts (#5, #9) get a "Last reviewed" date and an annual review.
